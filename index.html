About the Club

The University of Cincinnati Cricket Club, established in 1989, is dedicated to promoting cricket and nurturing talent at all levels.

We are based in Cincinnati, Ohio and aim to promote cricket within the local and student communities. We welcome players of all skill levels, encouraging inclusivity while keeping a competitive spirit.

We play regional leather ball cricket tournaments in Midwest region (OH, IN, and KY), like the Midwest Cricket Tournament (MCT), and compete with teams across the region.

Highlights

Our achievements include:

  • 2025 MCT T40/T20 Playoffs
  • 2024 MCT T35/T20 Playoffs
  • 2023 MCT T20 Runners-up
  • 2021 MCT T35 Runners-up
